  2. Demos to help you understand the functionality of the platform

All responses are anonymous. To protect your anonymity, your name will not be collected or attached to any data. You will be identified instead with an ID code. myStrategyn is your JTBD and ODI platform aimed at helping you learn and build the capability of Outcome-Driven Innovation for you and across your organization.

The platform has three key components

Community

A community of like-minded individuals and organizations that want to communicate with others like yourself to keep up to date on the latest best practices.

Training

Courses designed at progressive levels to help understand Outcome-Driven Innovation and how to apply it within your role.

Tools

Tools to help you practice and apply Outcome-Driven Innovation.

Let’s look into each of these components in more detail.

Community

Our community aims to gather like-minded individuals to discuss and share best practices as you deploy Outcome-Driven Innovation.

Key Features

  • Become aware of improvements that are being made to the Outcome-Driven Innovation method within and outside your organization
  • Share and get access to tools and templates that other ODI practitioners are using to help aid in your and their application of Outcome-Driven Innovation
  • Get your questions answered virtually by Strategyn experts.
Training

Courses designed at progressive levels to help understand the principles of Jobs-to-be-Done and Outcome-Driven Innovation and how to apply them within your role.

1. Introduction
Definitions/mindset – Introduce a new way of thinking about innovation
2. Fundamentals
Structure/rationale – understand why ODI will work for you
3. Associates
Guidelines and Rules – a model for adjustment, alignment, action
4. Practitioner
(IN DEVELOPMENT)
Execution – a script for putting ODI into practice

Key Features

  • Assess ODI against your current and other innovation methods and align your team around ODI as a common innovation method to help your organization succeed.
  • Each course level is built as a prerequisite to the one before it, so we’re building on the knowledge taught in the previous course.
  • As we develop more courses, they will be added to the platform and you will get access to them at no additional cost.
  • Badges and Certifications attached upon course completion that can be shared on your social networks
  • Monthly events focused on specific topics so you can meet with ODI practitioners, ask your questions, and get feedback on your work as they relate to those topics.

Tools

Our tools are designed to help aid in your practice and application of Outcome-Driven Innovation. We will be launching with the following three tools, and have an entire pipeline of future tools to add:

JTBD Needs Framework
Use the JTBD Needs Framework tool to help you quickly document and understand what type of customer, job, job steps, and outcomes to study based on your innovation goals.
Statement Checking
Check your job, job steps, and outcome statements to see if they fit all the guidelines defined in the Outcome-Driven Innovation process.
ODI Landscape Graphing
Graph your ODI Landscape by uploading the importance and satisfaction of your outcomes, and quickly understanding what the opportunities might be.

Key Features

  • As we develop more tools, they will be added to the platform and you will get access to them at no additional cost.
  • Each tool will record your data and store it so you can always come back and adjust it as needed.
  • Save as many projects as you want to in each tool.
  • Quickly understand the key concepts used in Outcome-Driven Innovation and use them daily as you apply them to your role with your organization.
